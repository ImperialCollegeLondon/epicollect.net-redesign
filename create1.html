Create Project

Please enter a name for your project - this will act as the name used for your website and also as the name used when entering into your phone to start collecting data.

Once you have created a project you can then design a form for data collection.

You can also amend the look and feel of your homepage by adding some explanatory text about your project and upload an image.

Please read the instructions prior to proceeding.
